About Us

ARCON Corporation provides specialized support in modern technology to the FAA, DoD, and other government agencies, as well as to business. ARCON has the resources to undertake projects ranging from a small, intricate subsystem of a project to the design of a complete system. Areas of expertise include: - Air Traffic Control Automation and Software Implementation - Tracking Analysis and Registration - Machine Learning in Air Traffic Control - Statistical Signal Processing, - Modeling, Simulation, and Data Analysis
